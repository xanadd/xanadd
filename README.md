## What I'm Building

**Softmarc LLC** is my small-business SaaS and web solutions company. Softmarc helps businesses launch credible websites, preview and purchase ready-to-customize templates, manage subscriptions and billing, and add the operational tools behind the site: customer portals, order panels, admin dashboards, automations, AI-assisted workflows, payments, bookings, lead capture, and system integrations.

The goal is simple: give small businesses software that looks sharp, works on day one, and can grow into the way they actually operate.
